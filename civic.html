Civic

The Honda Civic 2020 model is a compact car that comes in various body styles, including sedan and hatchback. Here are some key features and specifications: Key Features - *Engine*: The 2020 Honda Civic comes with a 1.5-liter turbocharged engine or a 2.0-liter inline-four engine, producing 180-205 horsepower. - *Transmission*: It has a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) or a 6-speed manual transmission. - *Fuel Economy*: The Civic has an estimated fuel economy of up to 32 MPG city and 42 MPG highway. - *Safety Features*: It includes advanced safety features like Honda Sensing suite, which comprises Collision Mitigation Braking System, Lane Departure Warning, and Adaptive Cruise Control. Specifications - *Dimensions*: The Civic sedan has a length of 182.7 inches, width of 70.9 inches, and height of 56.7 inches. - *Interior Space*: It offers 97.2 cubic feet of passenger space and 15.1 cubic feet of cargo space. - *Infotainment*: The Civic features a touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility. Variants - *Honda Civic LX*: The base model with standard features. - *Honda Civic EX*: A mid-level trim with additional features like LED headlights and fog lights. - *Honda Civic EX-L*: A higher trim with features like heated seats and a moonroof. - *Honda Civic Touring*: The top-of-the-line trim with features like navigation and a premium audio system. Price The price of a 2020 Honda Civic in Pakistan varies depending on the trim level and features, but prices for similar models range from PKR 8,329,000 to PKR 9,899,000.
